About Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. His father was the equipment manager for Andorinha, the local football club. Ronaldo first played for Clube Desportivo Nacional de Madeira and then transferred to Sporting Clube de Portugal (known as Sporting Lisbon).
